About US

We Take Care Of Your Healthy Life


Medcare is a Hospital Management System setup which is work for the smart hospital management system. The Hospital has a capacity of 300 beds with dedicated 8 Intensive / critical care beds, 3 ventilators, 3 BIPAPs , 3 private rooms, 6 semi private rooms, 8 economy rooms (general wards). Our 2 sophisticated operation theaters and 3 specific dedicated out-patient consultation rooms, and 2 emergency / treatment room complete the infrastructure of the hospital to offer the highest levels of medical care to our patients.

Medcare hospital provides wide array of services with most medical, surgical specialties as well as Digital X Ray facilities, physiotherapy, laboratory investigations and pharmacy available 24 /7.
